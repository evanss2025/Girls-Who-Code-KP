What is Girls Who Code?

Girls Who Code is a national club that encourages girls (but also everyone in general) to get more involved in computer science and coding!

We meet on Wednesday's (unless other specified) after school from 2:15PM-3PM.

We work on coding projects that anyone can learn!

We also find it important to give our members exclusive opportunities, so we also will be keeping track of hackathons and computer science competitions and programs, you can learn more by attending Girls Who Code!

FAQ

How do I join?

You can join by showing up to a meeting and then getting the google classroom code, here we post resources you made need for events and projects!

What kind of events are there?

We love to get members involved in outside computer science events, a big one are hackthons (coding competitions), some big ones of the year are Blueprint MIT and TechTogether!

What should I bring to meetings?

Make sure to bring a laptop (school chromebook is good!), everything else needed will be provided by us!

What do we work on at meetings?

Each meeting we will either start a new project or continue on one from a previous meeting, we will help guide everyone, so people may be at different places on projects.
